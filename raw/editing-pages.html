Editing Pages

 

Once you have created a page you can start to edit the content majority of it is self explanatory but here are a few things you should know:

 

1. This is the page name, once created you cannot change it.

2. This is the layout page, you can change this.

3. This is the page title (It will appear as a h1 tag)

4. This is the body template, the only time you need to un-tick this is if you are just rendering a widgets and not content.

5. Here is where you type your content.
